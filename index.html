About me

Background

I design thoughtful digital experiences that balance usability and clarity.

Hi, I’m Sinthia — a UX/UI designer with a Master’s degree in Computer Science specializing in Human-Centred Computing. I’m passionate about creating digital products that feel intuitive, accessible, and genuinely helpful for the people using them.

My work combines user research, systems thinking, and visual design to transform complex problems into seamless experiences. From redesigning grocery platforms and mentorship systems to exploring AI-driven and immersive experiences, I enjoy designing products that connect technology with real human needs. I believe great design should feel effortless — clear enough to guide users naturally without overwhelming them.

Outside of design, I’m inspired by storytelling, psychology, emerging technology, and everyday human behavior — influences that continuously shape how I think about user experiences.
